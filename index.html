Our Activities

Sports Team

Science Club

The Science Club meets every Wednesday after school from 3:30 PM to 5:00 PM. We conduct exciting experiments and explore various scientific concepts. All students interested in science are welcome to join!

Drama Club

The Drama Club rehearses for upcoming plays every Monday and Thursday from 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM. If you love acting or working behind the scenes, come join us and showcase your talent on stage!

Our school's sports team practices every Tuesday and Friday from 3:30 PM to 5:30 PM. We participate in inter-school competitions and focus on developing skills, teamwork, and sportsmanship.
